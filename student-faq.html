Course Registration
How do I add a course to my registration?v
Navigate to "Add a Course" from the sidebar. Review the requirements and visit the Registry Services Office with your request. Additions are only permitted within the first two weeks of semester.
Can I drop a course after the semester has started?v
Yes. Drops within Weeks 1–2 leave no academic record. Drops in Weeks 3–4 may have financial implications. After Week 4, a "W" (Withdrawal) grade is recorded on your transcript.
What happens to my fees if I drop a course?v
If you drop within the first two weeks, you may be eligible for a partial refund. After that period, the full tuition remains payable. Contact the Finance Office for your specific situation.
What is the deadline to add or drop a course?v
Course additions close at the end of Week 2. Drops without financial penalty also close Week 2. After Week 4, a Withdrawal grade is recorded. These deadlines are strictly enforced.
Credit Load
What is the standard credit allocation per semester?v
The standard allocation is 18 credits per semester. Taking more than 18 requires a formal Extra Credits request with a minimum CGPA of 3.0.
What CGPA do I need to apply for extra credits?v
A minimum CGPA of 3.0 is required. Students on academic probation are not eligible. The maximum overload typically permitted is 21 credits.
Programme Changes
How long does a programme change take to process?v
Programme change requests take up to 15 working days to process after submission.
Will my completed credits transfer if I change programmes?v
Credits may be recognised by the receiving department at their discretion. A credit recognition assessment is conducted as part of the review.
What documents do I need to change my programme?v
You need a formal motivation letter, approval from your current Faculty, and acceptance from the receiving Faculty. Submit everything to the Registry.
General
How do I track the status of my request?v
Track requests under "My Requests" in the sidebar — listed as Pending, Approved, or Denied with full details and any admin notes.
Who do I contact if my request is denied?v
You have the right to appeal within 5 working days. Contact the Faculty Dean's Office with supporting documentation and a written motivation.

FAQs — Quick Reference
How do I approve or deny a request?v
Navigate to "Pending Requests" from the sidebar. Each request shows the student's details, reason, and type. Use the Approve or Deny buttons to action each request.
What does the report page show?v
The Reports page shows total requests, status counts, a bar chart by type, a donut chart, and a full submissions table.
What request types can students submit?v
Students can submit four types: Extra Credit Load, Add a Course, Drop a Course, and Change Programme of Study.
